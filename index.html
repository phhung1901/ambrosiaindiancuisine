    Our Philosophy

     

    For a passionate individual, it is of immense source of enjoyment to feel at home anywhere. At Ambrosia such are some of the simple pleasures of intense, impartial and independent thinking minds. The ambiance is essentially created by the guests who frequent the restaurant. Through Ambrosia we share our passion of Indian cuisine and hospitality with you. Ambrosia encapsulates the dynamic culinary culture of Asian countries. This modern fine dining experience offers a suave, urbane space for you to enjoy Indian cuisine. Step in and grab your favorite drink and experience live dance and music as you indulge in delightful plates of food.
